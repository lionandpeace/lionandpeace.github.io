i am a 20 yr old creative based in nottingham and sheffield. i like to produce music, write articles and take and edit photos so here is my website where i can compile them altogether. i have (nearly!) finished my degree in philosophy at the university of sheffield, and will be taking a year out of an academic career to focus on my creative side which i felt i have neglected for a long time.

i am always interested in creative collaborations of all kinds, and all my material is free on a creative commons license. if you would like to contact me for any reason, feel free to drop me a line. x
